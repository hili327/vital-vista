What are Diseases?

A disease is a condition that affects the normal functioning of the body or mind. Diseases can be caused by a variety of factors, including infections, genetic conditions, lifestyle choices, or aging. In this section, we will explore some of the major disease categories: mental diseases, physical diseases, age-related diseases, and lifestyle diseases.

Understanding diseases is the first step in prevention and treatment. It is important to know how diseases affect different systems in the body and how we can manage or avoid them with healthy lifestyle choices.

Diseases can have varying symptoms and impacts depending on the affected system or area of the body. For instance, mental diseases may affect behavior and cognitive function, while physical diseases could impact vital organs or muscles. By recognizing symptoms early, individuals can take preventive measures and seek timely medical advice.
